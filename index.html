Hobbies I Began During Quarantine 2020

There are many things that wander through your head when you have nothing to do and nowhere to go. That's the position I found myself in during quarantine in March of 2020. I was furhloghed from work and everything was shut down. My fiance and I immediately stocked up on groceries and behaved as if we were on vacation. Whipee!!!!

FLORAL ARRANGEMENTS

After weeks of eating, making our own movies, eating, binge watching shows, eating....you see where this is heading, I turned on YouTube and that's when the light bulb in my head went off. I began watching videos on how to do many different things but landed on floral arrangements and resin art. I watched countless hours of diy floral arranging techniques as well as bouquets. After a few trips to the Dollar Tree and Walmart, I began making floral arrangements in the vases and pots I already had at home. I also went to a hobby store and bought some resin to use as faux water in my vases. Here are a few pictures of my very first floral arrangements done with faux flowers.

BOUQUETS

I stumbled into making bouquets because of pictures I posted of the floral arrangements I'd done. There's a lot of pressure doing bouquets, especially for a wedding because they have to be perfect. And of course my first one was for a wedding with live flowers. I had absolutley no experience working with live flowers, but I rolled up my sleeves and accepted the job. The bride was very pleased with my work and told me that it was exactly how she wanted it! I was elated at my accomplishment and shed a tear as I watched her walk down the aisle. Here's a picture of the bouquet and the boutennaire.

RESIN ART

As I began to research different types of resin to use for floral arrangements, I saw several videos on resin art and decided to take a look. I soon discovered that this was a rabbit hole that I could not escape. There's so many different things you can do artisticly with resin. I purchased some resin and molds, and began my journey into the world of resin art. I now own hundreds of molds and have created more things than I know what to do with. I began showing off my creations online which turned into people making orders to purchase my art. Here are some pictures of a few of my pieces.
